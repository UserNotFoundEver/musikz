Who is Infinati?

Infinati from Las Vegas, is a powerhouse when it comes to bass and psychedelic music. He has found a way to blend the two styles so you can have an entheogenic journey, or dance like a maniac. In all his performances, he strictly plays original music only, as his discography is stacking up. He has played major music festivals such as Shambhala with well-known acts such as Excision, Marshmello, and many more. Infinati blends bass, psychedelic and EDM genres in general.
