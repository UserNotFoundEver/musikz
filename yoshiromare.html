YOSHIRO MARE, an acclaimed 432hz producer and DJ, hails from the vibrant city of Cali, Colombia. With a robust educational background from Full Sail University and certification from EAS Colombia, YOSHIRO has honed his craft to perfection.

Over the years, YOSHIRO has made a significant mark in the global music scene with over 22 releases across prestigious international labels in countries such as Germany, Spain, Italy, Russia, Belarus, Ukraine, and his home country, Colombia. His journey reflects a relentless passion for music and a dedication to pushing the boundaries of electronic sound.

Currently, YOSHIRO MARE is pioneering a new path by releasing his music and art onchain, integrating his art with cutting-edge technology. By aligning with forward-thinking communities that resonate with his core values, YOSHIRO MARE continues to innovate and inspire, driving the future of music in an ever-evolving digital landscape.
